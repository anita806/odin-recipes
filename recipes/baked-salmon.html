Baked Salmon

Healthy and Easy Baked Salmon

Salmon is a great source of animal protein and an almost perfect source of omega-3s.It has a lot of protein, vitamin B, calcium, potassium, and antioxidants as well making it one of the best options for animal protein.

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillets
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Cracked black pepper
  • Minced garlic
  • Herb seasoning
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees and grease a large baking pan. Arrange the salmon fillets on the baking sheet and season generously with salt and pepper.
  2. Stir together the olive oil, garlic, herbs, and juice of 1/2 of the lemon.
  3. Spoon this sauce over salmon fillets, being sure to rub all over the tops and sides of the salmon so it has no dry spots.
  4. Bake the salmon in the oven for 12-15 minutes or until the salmon is opaque and flaky when pulled apart with a fork. You can broil the last 1-2 minutes if desired.
  5. Garnish with any herb of your choice.
